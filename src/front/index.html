The Airbus Vahana is an electric-powered eight-propeller vertical take-off and landing aircraft prototype financed by A³ by Airbus and Airbus Urban Mobility. Vahana project started in 2016 as one of the first projects at A³ (pronounced “A-cubed”), the advanced projects and partnerships outpost of Airbus Group in Silicon Valley.
